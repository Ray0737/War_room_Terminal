01. ASSET TARGETING

Open the Visibility Panel (Radioactive Icon) and enable Launch Deck. Select a missile card from the bottom slider. Click "SET TARGET" — the card will glow green. Click any location on the map to lock coordinates.

02. YIELD CONFIGURATION

Each unit can be equipped with different warheads. Use the Yield Selector on the card to choose between tactical and strategic loads. The Impact Radii (yellow/red circles) will adjust on the map to reflect the blast zone.

03. EXECUTION & ABORT

Once target is locked, press "CONFIRM". The missile enters IN-FLIGHT status. Telemetry will show Time-to-Impact. If the launch was accidental, press "ABORT" immediately to neutralize the asset mid-air.
